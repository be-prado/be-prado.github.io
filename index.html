ABOUT ME

My name is Bernardo and I'm a third year graduate student at the University of Michigan pursuing a Masters degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a Masters degree in Applied and Interdisciplinary Mathematics. I started at Michigan as an Applied Mathematics PhD student and have since grown more interested in Machine Learning, Signal Processing, Game Development, and Computer Graphics. I decided to use my PhD credits to get two Masters degrees and learn more about the subjects that interest me. You can scroll down to see my Video Game Development and Computer Graphics projects. At the very bottom of the page I list two of my Mathematics research projects. For my Machine Learning and Signal Processing projects, you can check out my GitHub linked right below this paragraph.

I am passionate about all forms of story telling, I love video games, movies, books and music. I enjoy making and listening to music and I am interested in how Machine Learning and Signal Processing can be used to make audio products better. I play the guitar for fun and I am trying to learn how to play the bass and the harmonica. I think that's enough about me. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn if you want to chat!

VIDEO GAME PROJECTS

  • PROJECT SKYSCRAPER This is my first project with WolverineSoft studio! Stay tuned for game details and dev blog updates!
    [DEV BLOG]


  • PARALLELIZED RAY TRACER This project implements Peter Shirley's Ray Tracing in One Weekend in C++, but it differs from the book's implementation in that the tracer was parallelized using CUDA. The parallelized code achieved a performance improvement of 12 times compared to the original code!



  • HUNGRY CAT COOKING Hungry Cat Cooking is a cooking platforming game where you will play as a cat that aspires to be the first feline to open a human restaurant! Of course, in the world we live in, a cat can't just randomly open a restaurant and serve humans. A long time ago, a fellow feline tried to accomplish such feat and wasn't successful. To your luck, this old cat created a training that will prepare you to thrive where he has failed. Check out the game trailer below!
