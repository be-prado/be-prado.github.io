ABOUT ME

My name is Bernardo and I'm a second year graduate student at the University of Michigan in the Applied and Interdisciplinary Mathematics program. I'm originally from Curitiba, Brazil and I enjoy reading books and comics, playing tennis and playing music. I'm also interested in developing video games, writing stories and learning about music composition. When it comes to math, I'm researching mathematical models of cancer and cancer treatment. I'm currently working on applying machine learning and statistical methods to learn a differential equation model of breast cancer from agent-based model data.
